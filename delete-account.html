How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Ivory OS account and all associated data, please send an email to:

wispersofthepastprints@gmail.com

Please include:

  1. Your registered email address
  2. Subject line: "Delete My Ivory OS Account"
  3. Confirmation that you want all data deleted

What will be deleted:

  • Your photographer profile
  • All client data
  • All bookings and invoices
  • Payment history
  • Account credentials

Timeline: We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

Note: If you have active subscriptions, please cancel them first in the app (Settings → Subscription) or contact us to cancel.
